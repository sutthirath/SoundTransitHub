About Sound Transit Hub

Why: There has been a growing trend in smart home devices known as “hubs,” such as the Amazon Show, Lenovo Smart Display, and recently the Google Home Hub. These hubs sit on your countertop and is “always on.” The devices display basic information such as time, date, and the weather with the added bonus of a smart assistant. The Problem: These hub devices have limited capabilities compared to a tablet or smartphone. Also, these devices are not affordable to everyone. The Solution: SoundTransitHub. A web browser application that displays real-time information for your local transit. This application has a “hub-like” interface and displays your personal local data. According to www.statista.com, over 1.2 billion already own a tablet. Simply go to SoundTransitHub.com and login to your personal account. The main screen will display your requested information with real-time updates.
